In Progress · Solo-Authored · Target: JAP / OBHDP

The Ability Penalty: How organizational AI adoption amplifies evaluation disparities between high- and low-performing employees

Full theoretical development, experimental design, and manuscript preparation

Does the mere presence of AI bias managers against low-ability employees even when objective output is held constant? Integrates attribution theory, reference-point shift, and task substitutability; three pre-registered Prolific experiments testing main effects, perceived substitutability as mediator, and task type as boundary condition.
